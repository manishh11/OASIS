Rohit Sharma: The Hitman of Cricket

Rohit Sharma, often referred to as 'The Hitman', is one of the most talented and successful cricketers in the world. Known for his elegant batting style and record-breaking performances, Rohit has become an inspiration to many aspiring cricketers.

Career Overview

Rohit Sharma made his international debut for India in 2007. Since then, he has become one of the most prolific run-scorers in the history of cricket. His ability to play long innings and his penchant for scoring big hundreds have made him a mainstay in the Indian cricket team.

Achievements

  • Highest individual score in a One Day International (ODI) match: 264 runs.
  • First player to score five centuries in a single World Cup (2019).
  • Most centuries in T20 Internationals by an Indian batsman.
  • Three double centuries in ODIs.
  • Led Mumbai Indians to multiple IPL titles as captain.
